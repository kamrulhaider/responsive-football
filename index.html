All Highlights Copa America 2021

Argentina became winners of the Copa America for the first time since 1993 as they defeated Brazil in a 1-0 encounter. The night belonged to neither Lionel Messi nor Neymar Jr as both failed to fire for their team. The only goal of the game was scored by Angel Di Maria.
